What to do when forest fire is detected ?

  • 01

    During a forest fire, you must be prepared to evacuate with your family at any time and to do so quickly when told. Follow these additional safety measures: Keep monitoring local radio stations and social media platforms for road closures, evacuation alerts and other up-to-date information. Close all the windows and doors in your home to reduce smoke and debris from entering and minimize fire damage.

  • 02

    Alert the authorities and call fire fighters.

  • 03

    If you are inside a forest during forest fire travel upwind and downhill. Move towards areas with little to no vegetation and away from canyons. Breath through a wet cloth. If the wind is behind the fire run in the direction perpendicular to the fire. If the wind is opposite to the fire run into the wind

  • 04

    Do not attempt to fight a fire under any circumstances; forest fires travel at incredible speed and you could put yourself at great risk
